# Customer Support Ticket Prioritization

## Business Goal

Improve response time and consistency by prioritizing support tickets based on urgency and customer tier.

## Scope

- Intake tickets from existing support channels.
- Assign priority levels: High, Medium, Low.
- Route tickets to appropriate resolver groups.

## Constraints

- Keep manual override available for support leads.
- Preserve audit trail for priority decisions.

## Success Metrics

- Reduce average first-response time by 30%.
- Increase SLA compliance to 95%.
